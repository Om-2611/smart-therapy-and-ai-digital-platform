# General Therapy Tools

Interactive, therapist-guided tools for emotional regulation and everyday mental wellness — usable across most concerns, no specialized diagnosis required.

---

### 🎡 Emotion Wheel
Helps clients name what they feel by drilling from broad emotions down to precise, granular ones.
Builds emotional awareness and vocabulary — the first step toward regulating difficult feelings.

### 🏠 Safe Space Builder
Clients design a personalized calming environment with soothing scenes, colors, and ambient settings.
A grounding tool they can return to anytime to feel safe, centered, and at ease.

### 🌊 Defusion River
Guides clients to watch their thoughts float by like leaves on a river instead of getting swept up.
An ACT-based exercise that loosens the grip of anxious or self-critical thinking.

### ⚖️ Thought Challenger
Walks clients through weighing the evidence for and against a distressing thought.
A core CBT technique that reshapes unhelpful beliefs into balanced, realistic ones.

### 🗺️ Micro Quest Board
Turns recovery into small, achievable daily "quests" that build momentum through action.
A behavioral-activation tool that fights avoidance and low motivation one micro-goal at a time.

### 🃏 Values Card Sort
Clients sort cards to discover what truly matters most to them in life.
An ACT exercise that clarifies personal values and inspires committed, meaningful action.

### 🏄 Urge Surfing
A timed, guided practice for riding out cravings and intense urges without acting on them.
A DBT mindfulness skill that proves urges peak and pass on their own.

### 🗄️ Worry Vault
Lets clients "lock away" worries and assign them a dedicated time to revisit later.
Contains rumination so anxious thoughts stop hijacking the rest of the day.

### 🔍 Facts vs Feelings
Clients sort statements into facts, feelings, and the stories their mind invents.
A reality-testing tool that separates objective truth from emotional interpretation.
